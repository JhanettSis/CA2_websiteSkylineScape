Terms and Conditions

Please read the following terms and conditions carefully before using our services... This privacy policy explains what personal data we collect from you and why. We follow the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK Data Protection Act 2018. Personal data is any information that identifies you. Where you wish to make a booking, the personal data we need to collect and process is likely to include;

-Names of all persons travelling

-Contact details (such as telephone number, postal and e-mail addresses) of the person making the booking

-Passport information of all persons travelling

-Travel insurance details for all persons travelling

-Dietary information where applicable

-Date of birth

-Emergency UK contact / next of kin details (names and telephone numbers) for all persons travelling

-Credit/ debit card or other payment information for the person making the booking.

We may share your data with suppliers.

Airlines Transport, Hotels and the providers of other forms of accommodation

Tour managers and guides, Excursion and activity operators

Travel insurance providers

Credit card companies / banks in respect of payments, Google - we work with third party company Google to automatically collect information

Your data is protected, and we only keep it for as long as necessary. You can withdraw your consent for marketing communications at any time. If your data is incorrect, you can request changes. You have the right to know what data we hold and can ask us to delete it in certain situations. We also use cookies on our website to improve your experience.
